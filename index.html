About us

Welcome to our handmade knitted and crochet stuff online-shop! First of all let us introduce ourselves – we are two sisters from Ukraine, Mariya and Olga. And we’d like to show you how our Mum, Liliya, is gifted. She has been knitting since she was a teenager. She really-really adores it. The ocean of love and part of her soul are in every thing our Mum made. We have been watching her work for all our lives and it’s so beautiful and powerful for us that we join her in making different knitted and crochet stuff. We are sure these photo are more eloquent than any word. And we also hope that you’ll decide to buy something for you or for your family, friends, colleagues, etc. You are warm welcome to our Instagram to see more goods. Best wishes, Liliya, Maria and Olga.
